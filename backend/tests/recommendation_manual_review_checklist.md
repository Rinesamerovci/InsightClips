Sprint 10 Manual Review Checklist

Purpose:
Validate that recommendation ranking and explainable insight summaries feel reasonable on real podcast data before the final demo.

How to run:
1. Upload and process at least 3 real podcast episodes.
2. Open the clips page and analytics page for each episode.
3. Inspect the top 3 recommendations returned for each podcast.
4. Compare the recommendation reasons and ranking factors against the transcript/subtitle content.
5. Mark the checklist below with the reviewer names and date.

Checklist:
- The first recommended clip feels like a clip a human editor would publish next.
- Recommendations do not cluster around near-duplicate transcript angles unless the source episode genuinely repeats the same theme.
- Insight summaries mention real ranking signals such as virality, hook quality, duration fit, or publish readiness.
- Ranking factors are understandable without reading backend code.
- Search results for at least 3 test queries surface clips whose titles/transcripts clearly match the query.
- Published clips can still appear, but unpublished ready clips are surfaced first when they have stronger upside.
