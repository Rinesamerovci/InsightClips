Sprint 3 Manual Review Checklist

Purpose:
Validate that the highest-scoring analysis segments match the team's subjective highlight judgment on real podcast episodes.

How to run:
1. Upload and analyze at least 3 real podcast episodes.
2. Open the dashboard and Supabase `scores` table.
3. Review the top 3-5 scored segments for each episode.
4. Mark the checklist below with the team.

Checklist:
- The top scored segments feel like real highlights a human editor would choose.
- The segments are thematically coherent and do not cut across unrelated ideas.
- The transcript snippets match the emotional or informational hook of the moment.
- At least 3 of the top 5 segments per long episode feel clip-worthy.
- The segment timestamps look correct when compared with the source audio/video.
